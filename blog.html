Line Follower Robot (LFR) is a simple autonomously guided robot that follows a line drawn on the ground to either detect a dark line on a white surface or a white line on a dark. The LFR is quite an interesting project to work on! In this tutorial, we will learn how to build a black line follower robot using Arduino Uno and some easily accessible components.
